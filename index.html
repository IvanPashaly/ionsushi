What about history of sushi and rolls?

The history of Sushi began in paddy fields in China, where fish was fermented with vinegar, salt and rice, after which the rice was discarded. The dish is today known as narezushi, and was created in Japan around the Yayoi period. In the Muromachi period, people began to eat the rice as well as the fish. During the Edo period, vinegar rather than fermented rice began to be used. In pre-modern times and modern times, it has become a form of food strongly associated with Japanese culture.
